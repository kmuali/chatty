Sir Tim Berners-Lee ..

Quick Tip .. !
He is an English computer scientist best known as
the inventor of the World Wide Web.

He is a Professorial Fellow of Computer Science at
the University of Oxford and a professor at the Massachusetts
Institute of Technology (MIT).

Berners-Lee proposed an information management system on 12 March 1989,
then implemented the first successful communication between a Hypertext
Transfer Protocol (HTTP) client and server via the Internet in mid-November.
